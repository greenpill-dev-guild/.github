# Marketing — Team Charter

> Tell the guild's story: campaigns, content, and brand.

**Key:** `MAR` · **Cycles:** none (brief-driven) · **Panel:** afo, nansel, kit

## Purpose

Linear team description (paste verbatim — the settings field caps at ~255 characters, and this version fits):

> Accepted marketing and creative briefs: campaigns, content, announcements, and brand assets. Every brief names its audience, deliverable, and where it ships. Product/UX design stays on Product; community relationships stay on Community.

## What belongs here

- Campaigns and coordinated pushes (Octant Epoch 12 + Artizen Season 6 campaign, MAR-12).
- Content production: video guides (MAR-1), newsletters (MAR-14), social, showcases.
- Brand and visual identity work (GreenWill badge brand identity package, MAR-15). `activity:design` on this team means brand and creative design.
- Announcement and launch communications for any guild product.

## What doesn't

- Product and UX design → Product.
- The funding pipeline a campaign supports → Growth (the campaign links the award or round, never tracks it).
- Community relationship work the campaign feeds → Community.

## Workflow states

Backlog · Todo · In Progress · In Review · Done · Canceled · Duplicate

- **In Review** is the creative review step: a deliverable is reviewed against the brief's audience and message before acceptance.
- Deliberately no Triage: briefs are scoped on purpose, raw ideas stay in Discord until accepted. No state changes needed; this set matches the live Linear configuration.

## Cycles

None. Marketing runs on briefs and campaign windows (epochs, seasons, launches), not sprint cycles. Due dates track the windows; the delivery-hygiene routine chases slippage.

afo, nansel, kit (kit rejoins with the Linear seat re-granted at the 2026-07-13 capital sync; his Q3 stipend lane is Green Goods marketing presentations). Roster is confirmed in the Evaluator Panels Linear Document.
