## What belongs here

- Cohort building and readiness (PGSP first-cohort readiness COM-8, Builder Space kickoff prep COM-9).
- Garden and operator onboarding and engagement (commitment-pooling operator onboarding COM-3, garden survey COM-7).
- Support coverage as a continuous role (tracked as a monthly coverage issue, see the compensation playbook).
- Gatherings, workshops, and community events.

## What doesn't

- The product features a cohort needs → Product.
- Announcements, campaign content, and creative assets for community pushes → Marketing.
- Partner or funder relationship management → Growth.

## Workflow states

Backlog · Todo · In Progress · In Review · Done · Canceled · Duplicate

- Deliberately **no Triage**: community work is created on purpose (from calls, cohort plans, or briefs), not harvested from raw reports. If community intake ever starts landing here automatically, add Triage then.
- No state changes needed; this set matches the live Linear configuration.

## Cycles

2-week cycles (cycle 1 started 2026-07-16), sharing Product's rhythm so cohort work and the product work it depends on can be planned together.

## Evaluator panel

afo, nansel, matt. Roster is confirmed in the Evaluator Panels Linear Document.

## Templates

From [linear-templates.md](../linear-templates.md), create native Linear team templates for:

- **Brief** — the default for scoped community work (a survey, an onboarding push, an event).
- **Continuous role** (a Linear Document, not an issue) — support coverage and other ongoing arrangements.

## Surfaces

- **Discord:** `#community` (weekly synthesis excerpt, community coordination).
- **Routines:** delivery-hygiene, weekly synthesis.
- **Common labels:** `activity:community`, `protocol:*` of the product the work supports (for example `protocol:pgsp` for cohort work, `protocol:green-goods` for garden onboarding).
