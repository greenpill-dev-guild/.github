## What belongs here

- The entire grant lifecycle, in every state: prospects (GROW-1, GROW-3, GROW-4), drafting, submitted, and active awards (GROW-13, Octant Epoch 12). One issue per opportunity, `Grant:` title, one `funding:*` label mirroring the lifecycle.
- Partnerships and BD follow-through.
- Funder-facing reporting and impact evidence coordination (the Impact Reporting project spans this team).

## What doesn't

- The delivery an award funds → Product, linked back to the award issue here.
- Campaign creative for funding pushes (an Octant/Artizen campaign like MAR-12) → Marketing, linked, never duplicated.
- Research that informs a proposal → Research, unless it exists only to draft the proposal (then it stays on the grant issue).

## Workflow states

Backlog · Todo · In Progress · In Review · Done · Canceled · Duplicate

- **Pipeline stage is the `funding:*` label, not the state.** States describe whether someone is actively working the opportunity; `funding:prospect → drafting → submitted → active-award` describes where the opportunity is. Keep both honest.
- The pipeline view is a saved view grouped by `funding:*` label (see the UI checklist; the old "Grant Scouting" project is retired, do not resurrect it).
- Deliberately no Triage: the grant-scout routine files prospects directly with the label and a decision-needed block. No state changes needed; this set matches the live Linear configuration.

## Cycles

None. Funding work is deadline-driven (program deadlines, epochs, rounds), not cycle-driven. Due dates and the pipeline view replace cycle planning; the delivery-hygiene routine chases past-due and stalled owned issues here like everywhere else.

## Evaluator panel

afo, matt. This is the thinnest panel; add a third member when one re-engages. Roster is confirmed in the Evaluator Panels Linear Document.

## Surfaces

- **Discord:** `#funding` (grant-scout digest, funding coordination).
- **Routines:** guild-grant-scout (files and dedupes the pipeline weekly), delivery-hygiene (non-funding BD issues only; the grant pipeline itself is grant-scout's turf), weekly synthesis.
- **Common labels:** `funding:*` (the lifecycle axis), `activity:capital` (monetization work), `protocol:*` of the product being funded.
