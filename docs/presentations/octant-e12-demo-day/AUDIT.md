# Design audit: GPDG Octant Epoch 12 deck

Multi-agent adversarial review (5 dimension reviewers + synthesis) of the current `index.html` against the Greenpill Dev Guild design system.

## Verdict

The muddy lime-to-forest gradient the user hates is the loud symptom, but the disease is deeper: this deck is on-palette and off-brand, wearing GPDG tokens as a costume while throwing away the three things that make the Guild unmistakable: the hand-drawn fantasy map (sitting unused in the deck's own `assets/brand/` folder), the persistent pill hero mark, and the parchment-and-forest rhythm. Roughly thirteen of sixteen slides are the identical flat Cal Poly Green field with one left headline, one lime sub, and a cold bespoke SVG exiled to the opposite corner, so the whole thing reads as a generic Web3 dark deck rather than the warm, mythic, parchment-grounded Greenpill world. The single biggest lever is to stop hand-rolling weak custom patterns and adopt the house deck system that already ships in `slides/index.html` (section dividers, two-column-plus-map, color-coded stack, badge-disc card grids, pill callouts, forest-or-parchment surface rotation), then place the fantasy map and bring back parchment so the deck travels.

## Ranked fixes

1. **[P0] Kill the muddy custom gradient (cover + close).** Delete the hand-rolled `.s-gradient` (`linear-gradient 135deg #1F3D1F, #2d5a2d, #5b8a1f`); `#2d5a2d` and `#5b8a1f` are in no token. Use flat Cal Poly Green or Forest Deep carrying the `.bg-pill-watermark` texture, matching `brand-guidelines-page.png`. If any gradient is wanted, use `--gradient-forest-pulse` (`#1F3D1F` to `#C2FF1A`) exactly, contained behind the pill mark or as a corner bloom, never a full-bleed wash.
2. **[P0] Adopt the house deck archetypes instead of bespoke flat-forest slides.** Map content to the patterns shipping in `slides/index.html`: rebuild slide 5 as the color-coded `.s-stack` on parchment; rebuild slides 2 and 9 capability sets as the `.s-services` card grid on `#254D32` (3 columns, radius 24, glow disc top-left); add one or two `.s-section` dividers (240px spring numeral over 96px creme label) to break the 16 slides into acts. Alternate forest, parchment, and divider so no two consecutive slides share a silhouette.
3. **[P0] Place the hand-drawn fantasy map and restore parchment beats.** `assets/brand/capital-journey-map.png` is referenced zero times. Give it a hero or quest section-divider slide on a parchment frame (radius-xl 28px, 2px Cal Poly Green border); it literally tells this deck's capital-journey story and carries Octant in its Blockchain Space realm. Separately, convert three or four content slides to `--color-parchment` using `s-light` tokens so the deck breathes toward roughly 60/40 forest-parchment.
4. **[P0] Redraw the dissolved world map; do NOT paste the allegorical fantasy map onto it.** Slide 7 continents are `#2c4f2c` at 0.55 on `#254D32`, a ~3% value delta, so they read as invisible coffee-stain blobs and the 14 pins float in undefined space. Redraw a real, recognizable simplified world map in the fantasy-map style: parchment/creme ground, hand-drawn wobbly coastlines, pastel lands (mint, alice-blue, creme at 60 to 80 percent), spring pins with `--glow-spring-sm`, keep the badge-disc legend. The `capital-journey-map.png` asset is allegorical crypto-geography, so it would be wrong on a real-cities slide; it belongs only on the quest divider.
5. **[P0] Fix the corner-exiled diagrams and the floating-center text.** Slides 11 and 12 absolute-pin the ring and flywheel to the page edge while text hugs the opposite corner, leaving 50 percent of the canvas a dead diagonal void. Replace with the DS two-column grid (`inset:140px 80px 100px; grid-template-columns:1fr 1fr; gap:80px; align-items:center`) so text and visual share one structure. Remove `justify-content:center` from `.pad` so short slides (1, 4) stop floating; anchor every headline to one shared top baseline.
6. **[P1] Restore the pill mark as persistent chrome and the missing type registers.** The pill hero shape appears once (cover). Add the `.chrome-pill` mark (36x60px, 3px spring outline, rotate 20deg, inner spring dot) top-right on every content slide; swap the off-brand teardrop placeholder `.pin` for the capsule silhouette. Introduce section-divider numerals and a centered Space Grotesk pill-callout with `--glow-spring-lg` for the two thesis lines (slide 3, slide 13) so peaks differ from filler.
7. **[P1] Cover accent, oversized body, tag alignment.** Apply `em.accent` to a word in the cover and close headlines (accent "Guild", accent "Greenpill"). Drop the inline `font-size:30px` on the cover sub (breaks the never-Satoshi-above-28px rule); standardize `.sub` to 22 to 24px, cap measure at ~45 to 50ch, and cut slide 4's five-line wall into two short declaratives. Align `.tag` to the token (12px, 0.08em).
8. **[P2] Encode warm/cool semantics in diagrams; design the empty media states.** Every diagram is lime-on-forest, so nothing differentiates diaspora from global funders from ownership. Introduce a restrained semantic: spring/steady-flame for the Guild's own layer, mint and alice-blue for partners and diaspora, creme for global funders, cryola for blocked/risk only. Fill chips with low-alpha land colors instead of all-lime outlines. For missing photos, replace the forest-on-forest dashed slot printing a file path with a Forest-Deep panel carrying `.bg-pill-watermark`, and plan a forest duotone or Forest-Pulse-tinted scrim for real imagery. Move slide 13's caption clear of the centered headline.

## Target direction

The redesigned deck should feel editorial, warm, and mythic-but-modern, unmistakably the Greenpill Dev Guild fellowship world rather than a crypto dark-mode template. It travels through a designed surface rhythm of roughly 60/40 forest to parchment: statement beats on near-black Ink or Cal Poly Green with the spring pill watermark for switched-on texture, system and diagram beats on Forest Deep, and product and proof beats on warm Parchment with forest-ink art and Steady Flame accents, with one or two parchment section dividers carrying a 240px spring numeral over a creme label to break the talk into acts. Type keeps Space Grotesk for display and Satoshi capped at 24px for body, but earns peaks and valleys: the two thesis lines land as centered Space Grotesk inside a glowing spring pill callout, the cover and close carry a single spring-green accent word, and one celebratory beat (Let's Fucking Grow) may shift to Clash Display or a Daybreak creme-to-spring accent so it visibly crests. The pill hero shape returns as persistent top-right chrome on every slide and as a glowing hero element on cover and close. The hand-drawn fantasy map anchors a quest or capital-journey divider on a parchment frame, while the real-gardens map is redrawn in that same hand-drawn cartographic style, parchment ground and pastel lands and glowing pins, never the allegorical asset pasted onto real geography. Diagrams are built from house primitives (the color-coded App/Social/Protocol/Blockchain stack, badge-disc card grids with glow on dark, a single de-cluttered flywheel) with a restrained warm-cool palette so spring marks the Guild, mint and alice-blue mark partners and diaspora, creme marks global funders, and cryola is reserved for the one blocked path. Glow is spent with discipline on dark only, gold and Clash Display are reserved for the single tonal crescendo, and every slide composition fills the frame as one structure rather than two islands across a void.
