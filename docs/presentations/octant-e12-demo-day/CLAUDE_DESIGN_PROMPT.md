BRAND TRUTH TO HONOR
Palette: deep forest as the steady ground (Cal Poly Green #1F3D1F and Forest Deep #254D32), Spring Green #C2FF1A as the switched-on signal used sparingly for accents, headlines on dark, glow, and the pill, warm Parchment #FFF8E1 and Creme #FCECA6 as the warm half of the brand, plus the pastel land colors Mint #B1FDEB, Alice Blue #DCEDFF, and Cryola #FF6978, and Steady Flame #7DC400 for green text on light. Ink #0F1F0F for the darkest statement grounds.
Type: Space Grotesk for display, headlines, eyebrows, and tags (bold, mythic). Satoshi for body and captions, never above 24px in this deck. JetBrains Mono for meta labels and technical tags. Clash Display reserved for one or two true hero crescendos only.
Signature moves that MUST appear: the pill capsule hero shape (999px radius) as a glowing hero element on cover and close AND as persistent small chrome top-right on every content slide; the pill-watermark texture (low-opacity ~7 percent spring capsules tiled on a 12 degree diagonal over forest) on dark hero and diagram grounds; the hand-drawn fantasy-map illustration as connective tissue; badge-disc icons (64px spring-filled disc holding a 2px Cal Poly Green line icon, with spring glow on dark); the color-coded stack diagram (stacked rounded layers, each a different land color, with mono uppercase meta tags); and a deliberate forest-to-parchment surface rhythm of roughly 60/40 so the deck breathes.
Voice: mythic-but-modern, Title Case headings, UPPERCASE letter-spaced eyebrows, short declarative body, one idea per sentence. Confident, communal, warm.

HARD CONSTRAINTS
Forbidden: any muddy multi-stop gradient, especially the dark-green to olive to lime ramp the current draft uses (no invented olive midtones like #2d5a2d or #5b8a1f). The only sanctioned dark gradient is Forest Pulse, Cal Poly Green straight to Spring Green, and even that should be contained, not a full-bleed wash. Do not paste the existing allegorical capital-journey fantasy map onto the real-world gardens slide; that asset is allegorical crypto-geography and belongs only on the quest or journey divider. Do not make thirteen near-identical flat-forest slides. Do not float short text in the vertical center of an empty field. Glow on dark surfaces only, never on parchment. No frosted glass, no bouncy physics, no emoji.
