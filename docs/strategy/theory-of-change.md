# Theory of Change & Vision

> Canonical strategy doc. Near-static: reviewed yearly at most. Translated from the Charmverse workspace (exported to Drive April 2026; Charmverse retired). A Linear reference doc on the ops shelf points here.

## Theory of Change

**IF** we focus our engineering and research resources on building standardized onchain impact reporting and Ethereum-native capital formation tools (DeFi, staking, tokenization),

**THEN** local regenerative communities can transition from precarious, one-time grant dependency to sustainable "local endowments," where verified impact data actively attracts confident funding and generates ongoing yield,

**BECAUSE** we believe the root barrier to scaling public goods is not a lack of capital, but the lack of a trusted, transparent link connecting financial value directly to verified real-world impact.

## Vision

Provide the "Lego bricks" for the regenerative movement: anyone, anywhere can start a local impact project with a ready-made suite of funding, coordination, and reporting tools.

## The Regenerative Stack

- **Green Goods, the impact layer.** Brings biodiversity and ecological work on-chain: gardens, actions, MDR evidence (Media → Details → Review), attestations, and funder-legible reporting through the Greenpill Impact Framework (GIF).
- **PGSP, the capital layer.** Validator squads and staking infrastructure that route yield to public goods; operator-first relaunch under way toward Lido CSM v3.
- **GreenWill, the social layer.** Portable reputation and identity for contribution, support, and impact activity.
- **The public front door.** Builder Spaces, the Greenpill Network site, and campaigns that turn listeners into contributors.
- **Guild operations.** Five teams (Product, Research, Community, Growth, Marketing), stipend-backed contribution, and an agent-assisted operating system.

## The results chain

Strategy is not an initiative. Initiatives come from it.

- **Strategy** → quarterly editions as Linear documents on the `Guild Operating System` shelf (`Strategy — Q3 2026`, `Strategy — Q4 2026`, ...). A new edition each quarter; the series is the version history. Strategy is an operating artifact, not canon, so it lives next to the initiatives it governs.
- **Outcomes** → Linear initiatives (Lean Outcome Cards: Outcome + Success signals + Target).
- **Indicators** → each initiative's `Indicator Register` Linear document.
- **Outputs** → projects and issues.
